About Us

We offer all in one mobile store management App (POS App) for small store owners to manage day to day activities and custom mobile and web App development for Startups and SMEs.



Objectives

To understand the market in Sierra Leone and build utility mobile Apps; which different types of businesses can use to improve their business operations, and also to help other Startup and SME businesses who are trying to build solutions similar to our market.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be #1 solution company that develop and build digital solutions for Startup and SME in Sierra leone and across Africa, that are simple to use and can cover all the use-case needed for Startup and SME to accomplish, manage and grow.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide custom mobile and web App development for Startup and SME businesses and companies in Sierra Leone. With the aim of developing the ecosystem through our digital solutions; Ed tech, Fin tech and Pharm tech.

BACKGROUND

CJBEM SL LTD is a startup Tech company that was founded by Mr. Chernoh Yayah Jagitay (a pharmacist and an entrepreneur) on the 7th of may 2020. It was started on the 1st June 2020, with the idea of empowering small start up and SME businesses through the use of technology that is a mobile solution. We are a software company with experienced and talented professionals. We went live in 2020, built two products and we are serving 100 plus clients. As a bootstrapped startup, we have earned more than $10,000 revenue in the last financial year 2021-2022. Presently we are the winner of September's “Pitch Night” competition 2022 , Theme: Exponential Technology, Digital/Industrial Disruptors/FinTech; Hosted by INNOVATION SL and their partners.
